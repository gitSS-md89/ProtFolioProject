The Best Movies According to Soumya Ranjan

Top 3 movies of all time

Interstellar

An eye opening movie of emotion, science, hope, time, universe and beyond. In a nutshell it's about life in the universe and an endless journey through time.

The Imitation Game

A mind blowing movie of an extra-ordinary scientist Honorable Prof. Mr. Alan Turing. Mind blowing performances by Mr. Benedict Cumberbatch, Mrs. Keira Knightley and others. Set in the time of World War II and the depicting how Prof. Alan created a computer to beat the mighty enigma machine.

Mahobatein

Hindi romantic movie lead by the King Khan. The Most romantic movie of all time. Check it out if you want!!!
